Alpha Prototype

For this prototype, I used the aggregate information available to create a map showing all of weapons exported by the united states since the year 2000. This gives an idea of the basic information that is available with this dataset.

For the final implementation of this prototype, I plan to use the more detailed underlying data to provide a wide range of interactivity. I'm hoping to use the year of each deal to provide additional dimensionality, either as an animation over time, or with a supplemental visualization that combines interactivity. I also plan to provide filtering capabilities, using the different types of weapons that the data in naturally split into. Finally, I will provide some details on demand, to allow the user to see some of the raw data.
