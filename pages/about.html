Why This Data

For this project, there were many datasets that I considered basing my visualization on. However, I chose this one for two key reasons:

  1. Historical Relevance: Because this dataset covers such an important topic, I feel that a visualization created with it will be relevant and highly informative.
  2. Detailed Data: Because there are so many facets and details to this dataset, there are a lot of of different visualization techniques that I can use to make it highly interactive.
